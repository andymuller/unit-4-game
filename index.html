Pokeball Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four Pokeballs below. By clicking on a ball, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the number displayed above the balls. You lose if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each Pokeball is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each Pokeball.
